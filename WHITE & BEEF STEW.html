HOW TO PREPARE WHITE RICE AND BEEF STEW

White rice and beef stew is a classic, comforting dish enjoyed worldwide. The recipe starts with cooking the beef until it's tender, usually with onions, garlic, and a blend of spices for a rich flavor. The stew may also include vegetables like carrots and potatoes. Simultaneously, the white rice is cooked until it's fluffy and soft. The key is to balance the flavors of the stew to complement the simplicity of the white rice. The result is a hearty, satisfying meal that warms you from the inside out. Whether it's a cold winter night or a simple family dinner, white rice with beef stew never disappoints. It's a testament to the magic that can happen when simple ingredients are transformed with care and passion into a dish that's more than the sum of its parts.

INGREDIENTS

1.1 lb trimmed boneless beef chuck, cut into thin strips

4.1/2 tsp salt

5.1/4 tsp black pepper

6.1 tbsp canola oil

7.1 onion, coarsely chopped
